# Travel Reimbursement Policy

## Stay

Maximum reimbursement:
$150 per night

## Food

Maximum reimbursement:
$50 per day

## Travel

Maximum reimbursement:
$75 per trip


## Receipts

Receipts are mandatory for all expenses above $25.

## Approval Rules

Claims above $500 require Manager Approval.

Claims above $1000 require Director Approval.

## Duplicate Claims

Duplicate submissions for the same trip are not reimbursable.
